About Us

Your accountant, not a faceless firm

We've been helping small businesses and start-ups across the UK since 2012. Here's what that looks like in practice.

Built for businesses that don't need a big four firm

Uppal & Co was founded with a simple idea: small businesses deserve the same quality of accounting advice that larger companies get, without the corporate overhead or impersonal service.

We work with sole traders, partnerships and limited companies across a range of sectors. Our clients include people just starting out who need help getting set up properly, and established businesses looking to cut costs and stay compliant without the hassle.

Based in Slough with clients across the UK, we combine local, accessible service with the flexibility to work with businesses wherever they are. Everything from your annual accounts to your day-to-day bookkeeping is handled by people who know your business by name, not by account number.

Certified ProAdvisor

We can set up, support and train your team on QuickBooks, helping you get the most out of cloud accounting.

How We Work

What you can expect from us

Plain English

We explain things clearly and avoid jargon. If something affects your business, you'll understand exactly what it means and what to do about it.

Fixed fees, quoted upfront

No hourly billing surprises. We agree a fee before we start, so you always know what you're paying. If the scope changes, we'll discuss it first.

Proactive advice

We don't wait for year-end to tell you something important. If there's a deadline approaching, a saving to be made, or a problem brewing, you'll hear about it early.

Accessible & responsive

When you phone or email, you get a real person who knows your business. We aim to respond within the same working day.

Want to see if we're the right fit?

We're happy to have an informal chat about your business and what you need. No obligation, no sales pitch.
