Privacy Policy

How we collect, use and protect your personal information.

Who we are

Uppal & Co Accountants (UK) Ltd is a company registered in England and Wales. Our registered office is 2 Copse Close, Slough, SL1 5DT. When we refer to "we", "us" or "our" in this policy, we mean Uppal & Co Accountants (UK) Ltd.

What information we collect

When you contact us via our website, phone or email, we may collect your name, email address, phone number and any details you provide about your business or accounting needs. We only collect information that is necessary to respond to your enquiry and provide our services.

How we use your information

We use the information you provide to respond to your enquiries, provide accounting and advisory services, comply with our legal and regulatory obligations, and keep you informed about relevant changes to tax law or our services where you have given us permission to do so.

How we store and protect your data

Your data is stored securely and only accessed by authorised members of our team. We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorised access, loss or misuse.

Sharing your information

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. We may share your information with HMRC or other regulatory bodies where required by law, and with software providers (such as QuickBooks) where necessary to deliver our services to you.

Your rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, ask us to correct or delete your data, and withdraw your consent to us processing your data at any time. To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at rohit@uppalaccountants.co.uk.

Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
